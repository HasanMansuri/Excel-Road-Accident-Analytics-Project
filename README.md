## Key Features of the Dashboard

### Casualty Breakdown
Breaks down fatal, serious, and slight casualties to understand the severity distribution of accidents.

### Vehicle-Type Analysis
Shows casualties involving cars, motorcycles, buses, trucks, and more, helping identify high-risk vehicle categories.

### Monthly Trend Comparison (2024 vs 2025)
Line chart comparing accident counts by month to reveal seasonal spikes and year-to-year changes.

### Road-Type Impact
Casualties categorized under different road types such as single and dual carriageways to assess infrastructure safety.

### Road Surface Conditions
Accidents distributed across dry, wet, and icy surfaces to understand the influence of weather.

### Area & Location Analysis
Casualty comparison between urban and rural regions to highlight geographic accident risks.

### Light Condition Effect
Distribution of accidents in daylight vs. darkness, useful for evaluating the need for improved visibility and lighting.

### Interactive Filters
Slicers for weather, area type, and date allow dynamic filtering and deeper insights tailored to user needs.

## Insights From the Dashboard
- Weather and road surface conditions significantly influence accident severity.  
- Motorbike and car accidents contribute heavily to total casualties.  
- Rural regions experience higher casualty severity due to speed and delayed response times.  
- Darkness increases serious accident rates, highlighting visibility challenges.  
- Monthly trends show seasonal variations that can guide preventive measures.
